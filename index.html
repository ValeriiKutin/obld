Benefits of Brazilian Lymphatic Drainage After Surgery
REDUCE SWELLING & BRUISING
It removes stagnant areas of lymph and cellular waste products and reduces inflammation in the affected areas, which can drastically decrease swelling and help bruises to heal more quickly. Reduction in inflammation can help with discomfort felt by many patients after surgery.
PREVENTION OF FIBROSIS (FORMATION OF SCAR TISSUE)
After a cosmetic procedure, the body has to create new connective tissue to heal the wounds created by surgery. When a lot of scarring happens after surgery, the tissue thickens and becomes tight. Scarring decreases naturally over time, but some scarring can be permanent and can affect the final appearance of the area after healing.
FASTER HEALING TIME
Patients heal more quickly when inflammation is reduced. The lymph system is also important to the body’s immune response and improving the movement of lymph can reduce the risk of infection after surgery.
